Таблицы:
EMPLOYEES (employee_id, first_name, last_name, email, phone_number, hire_date, job_id, salary NUMBER(8,2), commission_pct NUMBER(2,2), manager_id, department_id)
JOBS (job_id, job_title, min_salary NUMBER(6), max_salary NUMBER(6))
JOB_HISTORY (employee_id, start_date, end_date, job_id, department_id)
DEPARTMENTS (department_id, department_name, manager_id, location_id)
LOCATIONS (location_id, street_address, postal_code, city, state_province, country_id)
COUNTRIES (country_id CHAR(2), country_name , region_id)
REGIONS (region_id, region_name)

Если тип не указан явно, то столбцы *_id имеют числовой тип (за исключением строкового job_id),
 столбцы *_date - тип DATE, остальные столбцы - строковый тип
